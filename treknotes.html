Star Trek notes

  • Rigel

    There are two star systems that we've seen called Rigel.

    • "Near" Rigel

      This is the one that was on the way to the Klingon Empire in Enterprise, no more than a few dozen light years away. Also the one mentioned as the heading of the Doomsday Machine, with Earth "not far beyond". Star Charts calls this Beta Rigel. Its location has been shown on maps on screen, and it's supposed to be an A type star, so I've picked Tau-3 Eridani as the A type star closest to the location in the maps.

    • "Far" Rigel

      This is the one that we saw in the Original Series and in Mudd's Women, and this is the supergiant in Orion we call Rigel. It has the Kalar planet Rigel VII as well as the dilithium mining planet Rigel XII.

  • Deneb

    Similar to Rigel, there are two Denebs. The near one was mentioned in TOS, and the far one is Farpoint station from TNG.

    • "Near" Deneb

      Mentioned in TOS as Deneb II and V. Star Charts calls this Deneb Kaitos so I went with that.

    • "Far" Deneb

      is the supergiant Alpha Cygni, and has Farpoint Station.
